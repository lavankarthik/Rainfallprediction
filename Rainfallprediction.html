Introduction:

Rainfall prediction remains a serious concern and has attracted the attentionof governments, industries, risk management entities, as well as the scientiﬁccommunity. Rainfall is a climatic factor that aﬀects many human activities likeagricultural production, construction, power generation, forestry and tourism,among others.To this extent, rainfall prediction is essential since this variable is the one with the highest correlation with adverse natural events such as landslides,ﬂooding,mass movements and avalanches.These incidents have aﬀected society for years.Therefore, having an appropriate approach for rain-fall prediction makes it possible to take preventive and mitigation measures forthese natural phenomena.
